Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Bockle handles data for the mobile app published under bundle identifier com.billsteinbock.bockle.

Effective date: 2026-06-11 Tracking: Not enabled Collected personal data: None disclosed

Data We Collect

Based on the current app implementation, Bockle does not collect personal data such as name, email, contacts, location, photos, or financial information.

The iOS privacy manifest currently declares no collected data types and no tracking.

Data Stored On Your Device

Bockle stores game preferences locally on your device using on-device storage:

  • Timer duration preference (1 to 5 minutes).
  • Custom dice face configuration.
  • Timer expiration alert settings (mode, text, volume).

This data is used only to persist your settings and is not used for advertising or sold to third parties.

Network and Third-Party Services

The app is built with Expo and may contact Expo infrastructure for app updates and runtime delivery where applicable.

No custom user account system is present in the current source, and no in-app data submission endpoint is configured.

Your Choices

  • You can reset custom dice back to defaults at any time in Settings.
  • You can modify timer and alert preferences in Settings.
  • You can uninstall the app to remove locally stored app data from your device, subject to your platform backup settings.
